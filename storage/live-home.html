The overarching mission of ICE Publishers is to motivate children towards righteousness throughout their developmental years and provide them with guidance, life skills and moral support along the way.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    1. Is this a separate school subject, and how many lessons per year?

    Yes, this is a separate subject called Character Building. 40 lessons per year for classes 1-2. 50 lessons for classes 6-8. 55 lessons for classes 9 and 10.

    2. Will the students be graded in this subject, and how can we be sure it won’t be seen as another burden on the children?

    Characters cannot be graded as such. The purpose of this course is to enlighten, inspire, train and encourage children towards righteousness. Students will receive evaluating performance comments at the semester’s end with constructive criticism. Almost no homework is given except small assignments occasionally from grade 3. This course should not be seen as a burden but as an interesting “refreshment” due to its dynamic teaching approach, which involves the children on a personal level.

    3. Is it a requirement to start teaching this subject from Class 1 onwards?

    No. However, we recommend completing a Key Stage as it will support, further develop, and strengthen the taught ethics.

    4. Can a home-schooling parent teach the subject to their children at home?

    Yes. See details in the Home-schooling Link.

    5. Where can we buy the Urdu version?

    Please see our Urdu website. Www.characterbuilding.pk

    6. Can schools and educators receive guidance and supervision regarding teaching the subject and challenges with students?

    Yes, this will be included in the yearly package if you choose a membership package. Other specific inquiries regarding implementation can always be sent to us via e-mail or by contacting our customer service, and we will guide you step by step.
